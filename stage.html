Rulebook

1. EVENT OVERVIEW:

The Open Stage Event is a platform for participants to showcase their talents in various performing arts, including but not limited to music, dance, drama, and poetry

2. ELIGIBILITY:

The event is open to all college students Participants can perform individually or in groups

3. REGISTRATION:

Participants must register in advance to secure a slot in the Open Stage Event Registration forms will be available soon

4. PERFORMANCE GUIDELINES:

Each performance should be within a time limit Performances should be appropriate for a diverse audience and adhere to college guidelines

5. EQUIPMENT AND PROPS:

Participants must bring their own instruments and props Technical requirements (audio, lighting, etc) must be communicated in advance.

6. JUDGING CRITERIA:

Performances will be judged based on creativity, originality, technical skill, audience engagement, and overall presentation

7. PRIZES:

Prizes will be awarded to the top performers, including certificates or trophies

8. BACKSTAGE PROTOCOL:

Participants must be ready backstage at least 15 minutes before their scheduled performance time Backstage volunteers will assist with coordination.

9. PROFESSIONALISM:

Participants should conduct themselves professionally and respectfully towards organisers, judges, and fellow participants

10. DISQUALIFICATION:

Any violation of the event rules may result in disqualification Disruptive or inappropriate behaviour will not be tolerated.

11. AUDIENCE ETIQUETTE:

Audience members are expected to show respect and appreciation for all performers The use of mobile phones during performances is not allowed

12. PHOTOGRAPHY AND RECORDING:

Photography and recording are allowed with permission from the performers

13. DISCLAIMER:

The organising committee reserves the right to amend the rules or disqualify any participant for reasons not covered in this rulebook

14. CONTACT INFORMATION:

For any queries, contact volunteers

15. EVENT SCHEDULE:

The detailed schedule of performances will be communicated to participants before the event

Ready to Participate?

By participating in the Open Stage Event, participants agree to abide by the rules and guidelines outlined in this rulebook.!
