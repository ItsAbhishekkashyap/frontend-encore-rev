(Monoact)

Prepare yourselves for a rollercoaster ride of creativity, drama, and sheer brilliance. The Monoact Extravaganza is not just a competition; it's your ticket to showcase your individual acting prowess and steal the spotlight. Get ready to ignite the stage with your charisma and leave the audience in awe!

Rulebook

There will be 2 rounds for mono act. NOTE: CO-ORDINATORS OF THE EVENT COULD CHANGE THE RULES IF FELT NECESSARY.

1. ROUND 1 (Online Mode: Registration is free)

a. Time limit for the monologue videos is 1-3 minutes. b. Sound should be clear. c. Use of any offensive language is strictly prohibited. d. Script can be original or from any movie or play. ->SELECTED CANDIDATES WILL GO TO FINALE.

2. Round 2

a. Time limit for the act is 4-8 minutes. b. Exceeding the time limit will result in penalties. c. No pre-recorded music is allowed. d. Candidates have to bring their own props. e. Your act will be judged on: voice clarity, acting, stage pressure management, direction, costume, make-up, and the overall impact of your performance. Embrace the spotlight, let the Encore Monoact Extravaganza be your stage, and leave an everlasting impression!

3. Time Frame:

Each dancer will have a set time to dazzle the audience Time limit per round: 2 minutes

4. Judgement Criteria:

Creativity: Originality and uniqueness in dance moves Technique: Precision, control, and mastery of dance techniques Musicality: Synchronisation with the rhythm and musical nuances Style: Expression and personal flair that sets the dancer apart Showmanship: Engagement with the audience and stage presence

5. Fair Play:

Respect your opponent and the spirit of friendly competition Any unsportsmanlike behaviour may result in disqualification.

6. Stage Usage:

Use the entire stage to showcase your moves Props are not allowed in the one-to-one battle.

7. Song Selection:

Participants should be prepared to dance to a variety of music styles Adaptability is key to winning the dance battle.

8. Judges' Decision:

Judges' decisions are final and based on a combination of the above criteria The judging panel may include experienced dancers and choreographers.

9. Safety First:

Prioritise your safety and that of your opponent Avoid moves that may pose a risk to yourself or others.

10. Enjoy the Encore Solo Showdown:

Let your passion for dance shine through every movement Make the stage your canvas and the Encore Solo Showdown your masterpiece.

11. Rulebook Adaptation:

Rules are subject to adaptation for the smooth flow of the competition Any changes will be communicated by the organisers.

12. Cheers and Respect:

Applaud your opponent; acknowledge the energy and effort they bring to the dance floor This is a celebration of dance, so let the rhythm unite everyone May the Encore Solo Showdown be a dance battle to remember
